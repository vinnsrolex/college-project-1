RAGHU EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS

Raghu Engineering College is an engineering college located in the outskirts of the city of Visakhapatnam,Andhra Pradesh,India.Established in 2001,it is situated on NH-43,37 km away from Vishakapatnam and 15 km away from Vizianagaram.Raghu Engineering College and Raghu Institute of Technology has been sanctioned AUTONOMOUS status by UGC and both colleges are permanently affiliated to JNTU Kakinada. Facilities include a library,computer labs for various purposes,separate CAM and CAD labs program guided machine operations and sports complex.

Branches in RAGHU EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS are:

  • RAGHU ENGINEERING COLLEGE
  • RAGHU INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY
  • RAGHU COLLEGE OF PHARMACY

RAGHU ENGINEERING COLLEGE
